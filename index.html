The Gorilla Adventure.

The Eastern Mountain Gorillas are some of the rarest animals on the planet . Only 1000 are left in the wild mostly found in Uganda and they are very shy.These gorillas can weight upwards of 450 lbs. You would think something that big would be easy to find right? On this adventure you will find the answer.

You and two guides are at the beginning of the Mountain trail in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest National Park. Do you want to go forward?
